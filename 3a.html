3. Passive Voice (Cāu b? d?ng) ?

C?u trśc: Subject + to be (chia theo thģ) + past participle + (by agent)

  • Present Simple: am/is/are + past participle ? "Obento is prepared every morning."
  • Past Simple: was/were + past participle ? "A new menu was introduced last week."
  • Present Perfect: have/has been + past participle ? "Snacks have been removed recently."
  • Future (will): will be + past participle ? "A workshop will be organized next week."
  • Future (be going to): is/are going to be + past participle ? "A new area is going to be built."
  • Present Perfect + just: have/has just been + past participle ? "Rules have just been established."

Obento – Japanese School Lunch Culture

In Japan, a packed lunch called obento is carefully prepared by mothers every morning. Japanese mothers take great pride in making beautiful and nourishing boxes with pickled vegetables and fresh ingredients. Schools establish regulations to ensure the food is healthy. Magazines inspire parents with creative ideas so children – even picky eaters – enjoy their meals. Parents are looked upon as having a great influence on children's food habits at nursery school. If a mother runs out of ideas, she turns to cooking guides for help.
