Amazing balloon flight

Tom Morgan flew 25 kilometres across South Africa using only 100 party balloons. Why did he do it? 'I started an adventure club at my office,' he says. 'I think modern life is too safe. We wanted to show people that it's good to try new things, and we started doing lots of different sports together.'

Tom first had the idea for the balloon flight after reading about a man who went across the USA using similar balloons. However, he wasn't able to do it in his home country of Botswana because of problems with the weather. So, he came to South Africa.

During the flight, he was worried about many things. 'I didn't know how dangerous it was going to be,' he says. 'I was cold, and I was frightened. But I didn't think about that. I was just enjoying the flight!'

Now Tom is working on a new plan to start an international balloon race. 'I'd like lots of people to try it,' he says. 'It's great fun!'
