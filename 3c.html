Introduction to Restaurant Reviews

A restaurant review is a written evaluation of a dining experience. A good review provides useful information to help others decide whether to visit the restaurant. It typically includes details about the food, service, atmosphere, and value for money.

In this module, you'll learn vocabulary, phrases, and structures to write effective restaurant reviews in English.

3. Restaurant Review Structure
  1. Title (restaurant name) - Start with the restaurant's name
  2. Introduction: Where is it? When did you visit it? Why?
  3. Description: décor, atmosphere, food, service, prices
  4. Favorite dish: describe 1–2 dishes in detail
  5. Closing: your opinion / recommendation
  6. Rating: ★ to ★★★★★ (1 to 5 stars)

Example structure:

1. Title: Le Corto - French Fine Dining in District 1

2. Introduction: Le Corto is a famous French restaurant located on Nguyễn Siêu Street in District 1. I visited it last weekend with my family.

3. Description: The décor is warm and elegant, and the atmosphere feels very luxurious. The food is excellent, especially the Coq au vin and the creamy crème brûlée. The staff are professional and friendly, and the service is fast. Prices are quite high, but the quality is worth it.

4. Favorite dish: My favorite dish is the crème brûlée because it is smooth and perfectly caramelized.

5. Closing: This restaurant is perfect for special occasions.

6. Rating: ★★★★★

4. Model Example
Le Corto - French Fine Dining in District 1

Le Corto is a famous French restaurant located on Nguyễn Siêu Street in District 1. I visited it last weekend with my family. The décor is warm and elegant, and the atmosphere feels very luxurious. The food is excellent, especially the Coq au vin and the creamy crème brûlée. The staff are professional and friendly, and the service is fast. Prices are quite high, but the quality is worth it.

My favorite dish is the crème brûlée because it is smooth and perfectly caramelized.

This restaurant is perfect for special occasions.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5 stars)
Analysis of the Model Review:
  • Title: Includes restaurant name and location
  • Introduction: Answers who, what, when, where
  • Description: Covers décor, atmosphere, food, service, and prices
  • Favorite dish: Specific description with reasons
  • Closing: Clear recommendation for specific occasions
  • Rating: Clear star rating
Practice Tips
How to Write Your Own Restaurant Review:
  1. Choose a restaurant you've visited recently
  2. Take notes about your experience while it's fresh in your memory
  3. Follow the structure outlined in section 3
  4. Use descriptive vocabulary from section 1
  5. Be specific about what you liked or didn't like
  6. Give reasons for your opinions
  7. Be honest but fair in your evaluation
Common Phrases to Use:

Positive phrases:

  • "I was impressed by..."
  • "The standout dish was..."
  • "What made this experience special was..."
  • "I would definitely return for..."

Constructive criticism:

  • "The only drawback was..."
  • "One area for improvement would be..."
  • "I found the prices to be a bit high for..."
  • "The service was somewhat slow during..."
