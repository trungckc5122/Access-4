READING PART 2 – Three friends visit a museum
A
Julia

"I found the History of Fashion Museum by accident when I was walking around the city centre – it's in a small street behind the cathedral, and not easy to find. I couldn't wait to see the dress collection as I'm interested in all kinds of design, and it was amazing. There were videos of the latest fashions being made, and I enjoyed watching them. I've never seen anything like it in other museums."

B
Becky

"A museum guide told me about some great cafes near the museum, so I'll probably go back there soon for a drink. I liked the fashion exhibition, but there was another exhibition about firefighting that was more interesting because I learnt so much about the job. I went to the museum alone because I wanted to listen to the guides talking about the exhibits. I'd like to do that job myself one day."

C
Tania

"I've been to the History of Fashion Museum three times now. There was an exhibition about hat design that I loved, but most visitors went to see the shoe exhibition instead. I also went to one of the talks the museum offers for students, which was really good fun. We had to work in groups to design a poster and the guides were there to help us. I really enjoyed doing that."
