Super Senses in the Animal Kingdom

Animals have remarkable senses that often far exceed human capabilities. A falcon has such incredible vision that it can spot a mouse from hundreds of meters away. Cats use their sensitive whiskers to measure spaces, while snakes flick their tongues to detect scents. An octopus explores its surroundings with eight flexible tentacles, each covered in touch receptors. Birds chirp to communicate, and some animals even qualify as having "super senses". Scientists have conducted a lot of research to understand how these creatures perceive the world, yet there is still much we do not know. Everyone who studies animal behavior discovers something amazing.
