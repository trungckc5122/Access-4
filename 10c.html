Writing a Formal Letter: Describing Your Stay with a Host Family

Task
You recently went on a language course to the USA, where you stayed with a host family. Write a formal letter to the company who organised the trip, describing your stay. Include the following:

  • Say who you stayed with (the family members), and where you stayed.
  • Give two examples to show how the family looked after you well.
  • Mention one small problem with your room or with the house.
  • Say that you would like to thank the host family, and recommend them to others.
📌 Note: This is a formal letter. Use polite language, avoid contractions (don't → do not), and organise your ideas in clear paragraphs.
1. Formal Salutation and Closing

Start with: Dear Sir or Madam,

2. Structure and Style
  1. Chia lá thư của bạn thành các đoạn văn.
  2. Trong đoạn đầu tiên (có thể chỉ là một câu), hãy nói rõ lý do bạn viết thư.
  3. Mỗi đoạn văn nên có một ý chính. Tốt nhất nên trình bày từng yêu cầu của đề bài trong một đoạn riêng.
  4. Trong đoạn cuối (cũng có thể chỉ là một câu), hãy nói rằng bạn mong nhận được hồi âm hoặc bày tỏ lời cảm ơn.
  5. Tránh sử dụng ngôn ngữ thân mật và các dạng viết tắt (ví dụ: don't → do not, I'm → I am).
3. Useful Sentence Structures (Use these in your writing)

Opening – Purpose
• I am writing to inform you about...
• I would like to share my experience of...
• I am contacting you with regard to...

Describing the family and location
• I was hosted by the ... family, who lived in...
• The family consisted of...
• Their home was located in a quiet area of...

Describing how they looked after you
• They were extremely kind and supportive.
• For example, they ...
• Moreover, they ...

Mentioning a small problem
• The only minor issue I experienced was...
• I encountered a minor difficulty with...
• Fortunately, it was resolved quickly after I informed them.

Thanking and recommending
• I would like to express my gratitude for their hospitality.
• I would be grateful if you could pass on my thanks to them.
• I would highly recommend this host family to future students.
• I look forward to hearing from you in due course.

5. Model Example
Sample Formal Letter

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to share my experience during the language course in the USA, which was organised by your company last month. I had the pleasure of staying with the Johnson family in a quiet residential area in San Diego.

The host family consisted of Mr. and Mrs. Johnson and their teenage daughter. Their home was both comfortable and conveniently located, making it easy for me to commute to the language school each day.

They were exceptionally hospitable and did everything possible to make me feel at home. For example, they provided healthy and delicious meals every day, and Mr. Johnson regularly helped me practise conversational English in the evenings. Additionally, they took me on a weekend trip to the Grand Canyon, which was both educational and memorable.

The only minor issue was that the air conditioning in my room did not work properly on one or two occasions. However, once I reported the problem, it was quickly resolved.

Overall, I greatly appreciated their support and kindness throughout my stay. I would be very grateful if you could convey my thanks to the Johnsons, and I would strongly recommend them to any future students taking part in this programme.

Yours faithfully,
Trung

Phân tích bài mẫu
  • Đoạn 1: Lý do viết thư (experience with host family) + giới thiệu gia đình và địa điểm.
  • Đoạn 2: Thành viên gia đình và vị trí nhà.
  • Đoạn 3: Hai ví dụ cụ thể về sự chăm sóc (bữa ăn, luyện nói tiếng Anh, chuyến đi cuối tuần).
  • Đoạn 4: Vấn đề nhỏ (air conditioning) và cách giải quyết nhanh chóng.
  • Đoạn 5: Cảm ơn và đề xuất gia đình cho học sinh khác.
  • Ngôn ngữ: Trang trọng, không dùng từ rút gọn, bố cục rõ ràng.
6. Self-check
  • Mở đầu thư đúng kiểu formal (Dear Sir or Madam / Dear Mr/Ms...)
  • Kết thúc thư bằng Yours faithfully / Yours sincerely theo đúng quy tắc
  • Thư có ít nhất 4 đoạn rõ ràng, mỗi đoạn một ý chính
  • Đoạn 1 nói rõ lý do viết thư
  • Đoạn 2 mô tả rõ gia đình và nơi ở
  • Đoạn 3 nêu cách gia đình chăm sóc bạn (có ví dụ cụ thể)
  • Đoạn 4 trình bày vấn đề nhỏ một cách lịch sự
  • Đoạn cuối cảm ơn và đề xuất gia đình cho người khác
  • Không dùng từ rút gọn (don't, I'm...)
  • Không dùng ngôn ngữ quá thân mật
7. Additional Useful Expressions

Formal connectors to improve your letter:

  • Furthermore / In addition / Moreover – to add information (e.g., Moreover, they helped me with my homework.)
  • However / Nevertheless – to introduce a minor problem (e.g., However, there was a small issue with the heating.)
  • For instance / For example – to give specific examples of care.
  • Overall / In conclusion – to summarise and thank.

More polite phrases:
• I would be grateful if you could...
• Thank you once again for your assistance.
• Please do not hesitate to contact me if further information is required.
