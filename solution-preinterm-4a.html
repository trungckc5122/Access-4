Weather Adjectives

thundery /ˈθʌn.dər.i/: with thunder (có sấm sét).
cloudy /ˈklaʊ.di/: with many clouds (nhiều mây).
snowy /ˈsnəʊ.i/: with a lot of snow (có tuyết).
frosty /ˈfrɒs.ti/: very cold, with frost (giá rét, có sương muối).
foggy /ˈfɒɡ.i/: with a lot of fog (có sương mù).
icy /ˈaɪ.si/: covered with ice (đóng băng, trơn trượt).
stormy /ˈstɔː.mi/: with strong wind and rain (có bão).
windy /ˈwɪn.di/: with a lot of wind (có gió).
sunny /ˈsʌn.i/: with bright sunlight (có nắng).
rainy /ˈreɪ.ni/: raining a lot (có mưa).
sweltering /ˈswel.tər.ɪŋ/: extremely hot (nóng bức, ngột ngạt).
mild /maɪld/: not very cold (ôn hòa, không quá lạnh).
cool /kuːl/: slightly cold in a pleasant way (mát mẻ).
freezing /ˈfriː.zɪŋ/: extremely cold (lạnh giá, buốt).

Weather Nouns

thunder /ˈθʌn.dər/: the loud noise from the sky (sấm).
cloud /klaʊd/: grey or white shape in the sky (mây).
snow /snəʊ/: frozen water falling from sky (tuyết).
frost /frɒst/: white layer of ice on surfaces (sương giá).
fog /fɒɡ/: thick cloud near the ground (sương mù).
ice /aɪs/: water that has frozen solid (băng).
storm /stɔːm/: bad weather with wind and rain (bão).
wind /wɪnd/: current of air moving (gió).
sunshine /ˈsʌn.ʃaɪn/: light and heat from the sun (ánh nắng).
thunderstorm /ˈθʌn.də.stɔːm/: storm with thunder and lightning (bão kèm sấm sét).
raindrops /ˈreɪ.drɒp/: a single drop of rain (giọt mưa).

Listening Script

Transcript 1.14

1.
Narrator: Yesterday morning the weather was terrible. It started to rain just after breakfast and it only stopped at about one o’clock. But then the sun came out and we spent the afternoon sunbathing in the park.
2.
Narrator: When we drove to school this morning, it was really foggy. It was so thick that we could only see about 20 metres ahead. Not only that, the road was really icy and slippery because the temperature in the night was about minus ten degrees Celsius. The bus driver had to drive really slowly and carefully.
3.
Narrator: It was school sports day yesterday. Unfortunately the sky was quite cloudy for most of the day and the sun didn’t shine at all. It was quite mild and we heard some crashes of thunder, but luckily it didn’t rain.
4.
Narrator: I went to play football in the park with some friends on Sunday morning. It was a lovely clear day but it was freezing cold and very frosty. There wasn’t any snow but the grass was completely white! Then in the afternoon the weather changed. The wind started to blow and it snowed quite heavily. By the evening there was about 10 centimetres of snow on the ground.
