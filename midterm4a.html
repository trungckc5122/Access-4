Grammar & Vocabulary Reference
Personality Adjectives (Tính từ chỉ tính cách) ▼
sociable (hòa đồng)
enjoying being with other people and making friends
shy (nhút nhát)
feeling nervous or uncomfortable when meeting new people
sensitive (nhạy cảm)
easily hurt emotionally by what others say or do
honest (trung thực)
telling the truth and not cheating or lying
optimistic (lạc quan)
believing that good things will happen in the future
patient (kiên nhẫn)
able to wait calmly without getting angry
selfish (ích kỷ)
caring mainly about your own needs and not those of others
stubborn (bướng bỉnh)
refusing to change your mind even when it is sensible to do so
polite (lịch sự)
behaving in a respectful and well-mannered way
flexible (linh hoạt)
willing to change your opinion or plans easily

Phrasal Verbs (Cụm động từ) ▼
get by (xoay sở)
to manage to live or do a particular thing using the money or knowledge you have
Ví dụ: They get by on her salary.
take over (tiếp quản)
to begin to have control of something
Ví dụ: She took over the conversation.
get someone down (làm ai buồn)
to make someone feel sad or depressed
Ví dụ: Don't let his comments get you down.
boss someone around (sai khiến ai)
to tell someone what to do repeatedly and in an annoying way
Ví dụ: He bosses everyone around.
react to (phản ứng lại)
to act or behave in a particular way because of something
Ví dụ: She reacts strongly to criticism.
avoid (tránh)
to stay away from someone or something
Ví dụ: He avoids talking about his mistakes.
Nguồn: Oxford Learner's Dictionary
Present Simple & Present Continuous (Thì hiện tại đơn & tiếp diễn) ▼
Present Simple (HTĐ)
Cấu trúc: S + V(s/es) (he/she/it thêm -s/-es)
Dùng cho: thói quen, sự thật hiển nhiên, lịch trình cố định.
Ví dụ: She goes to school by bike. / Water boils at 100°C.
Present Continuous (HTTD)
Cấu trúc: S + am/is/are + V-ing
Dùng cho: hành động đang diễn ra tại thời điểm nói, sự thay đổi tạm thời.
Ví dụ: She is studying now. / He is always using my phone!
always + HTTD
Diễn tả sự phàn nàn về thói quen gây phiền.
Ví dụ: He is always playing loud music!
Nguồn: English Grammar in Use - Cambridge
Used to / Would (Thói quen trong quá khứ) ▼
used to + V (đã từng)
Dùng cho thói quen, trạng thái trong quá khứ (giờ không còn).
Ví dụ: I used to be shy. / She used to have long hair.
would + V (thường xuyên)
Chỉ dùng cho hành động lặp lại trong quá khứ (không dùng cho trạng thái).
Ví dụ: We would go swimming every Sunday. (KHÔNG nói: I would be shy)
Lưu ý quan trọng
- Chỉ used to mới dùng cho trạng thái (be, have).
- would chỉ dùng cho hành động lặp lại.
Nguồn: Advanced Grammar in Use
Gerunds & Infinitives (V-ing & To V) ▼
enjoy / don't mind / avoid / suggest + V-ing
Ví dụ: She enjoys reading. / We suggested taking a break.
decide / want / refuse / promise + to V
Ví dụ: He decided to leave. / She wants to become a doctor.
make/let + object + V (nguyên thể)
Ví dụ: He made me say sorry. / They let me go out.
had better + V (nguyên thể)
Khuyên nên làm gì đó.
Ví dụ: You had better finish your homework.
too + adj + to V (quá...để không thể)
Ví dụ: The box is too heavy to carry.
adj + enough + to V (đủ...để có thể)
Ví dụ: She is old enough to walk to school.
Nguồn: Cambridge Grammar
WH-Questions (Câu hỏi từ để hỏi) ▼
Cấu trúc chung
Wh-word + trợ động từ + S + V?
Who (ai)
Hỏi chủ ngữ/người.
→ Who is always borrowing Beth's clothes?
What time / When (khi nào)
Hỏi thời gian.
→ What time does the class start?
Where (ở đâu)
Hỏi địa điểm.
→ Where are they studying?
Why (tại sao)
Hỏi lý do.
→ Why is he late for school?
What (cái gì)
Hỏi vật/tên.
→ What did she buy for her English course?
