Camp Treetops

Camp Treetops is a summer camp in the mountains where teenagers go to experience life in the natural world. The camp started back in 1947, when an engineer called Fred Hamilton decided he wanted his children to experience the sort of childhood he'd had.

[ 16 ] ✕ But he felt today's young people were missing out, and that was when he thought of setting up Camp Treetops.

Camp Treetops is like a little village, where the teenagers who attend sleep in wooden houses and help look after the animals. They also spend time cooking, growing fruit and vegetables, and doing various practical tasks. [ 17 ] ✕

It isn't just the teenagers who benefit from going to the camp, however. Local students also get involved, and many become camp assistants during their holidays. [ 18 ] ✕

They tell them they want to work in the holidays, rather than just sitting around. [ 19 ] ✕

They might spend hours doing something and not be happy with the result – but that's fine, as long as they have a positive attitude. Then, whatever happens, they won't regret their decision to try new things. [ 20 ] ✕
