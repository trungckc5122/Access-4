4. 🎧 1.17 Listen to three people talking about their jobs. What are the jobs? Choose two adjectives from exercise 3 which you think best describe each job.
Audio 1.17:

Audio Transcript

Speaker 1: I work as a receptionist in a hotel. The job is OK. There are lots of different things I have to do, like deal with the public, check guests in and out, answer the phone, use a computer, and so on. So every day is different. The problem is, I have to work long hours, sometimes at night, so I'm absolutely exhausted when I go home.

Speaker 2: I'm an architect. I finished college last year and this is my first job. I love designing houses, creating something beautiful for people. I love working in a team and the other people in the office are great. But we all work very long hours. Luckily, I get quite a lot of money so it's worth it!

Speaker 3: I'm a programmer. I work for a software company in London. I love my job. I often have to solve quite challenging problems, which is difficult and takes a lot of time, but I really enjoy it. I love the feeling at the end of the day when I have solved a really challenging problem.

Job Adjective 1 Adjective 2 Giải thích
1
Đáp án: receptionist; varied, tiring
Bằng chứng: "I work as a receptionist in a hotel... there are lots of different things I have to do... So every day is different. The problem is, I have to work long hours... absolutely exhausted..."
Giải thích: Người nói làm lễ tân khách sạn ("receptionist"). Công việc phong phú ("varied") vì mỗi ngày một khác ("every day is different") nhưng mệt mỏi ("tiring") vì phải làm nhiều giờ và rất kiệt sức ("absolutely exhausted").
2
Đáp án: architect; creative, well paid
Bằng chứng: "I'm an architect... I love designing houses, creating something beautiful for people... Luckily, I get quite a lot of money..."
Giải thích: Người nói làm kiến trúc sư ("architect"). Công việc mang tính sáng tạo ("creative") vì yêu thích thiết kế nhà, tạo ra vẻ đẹp ("designing houses, creating something beautiful") và được trả lương hậu hĩnh ("well paid") vì kiếm được khá nhiều tiền ("get quite a lot of money").
3
Đáp án: programmer; challenging, rewarding
Bằng chứng: "I'm a programmer... solve quite challenging problems, which is difficult... I love the feeling at the end of the day when I have solved a really challenging problem."
Giải thích: Người nói làm lập trình viên ("programmer"). Công việc đầy thử thách ("challenging") do liên tục giải quyết bài toán khó ("solve challenging problems") và mang lại sự thoả mãn xứng đáng ("rewarding") khi đã vượt qua các vấn đề khó đó ("really enjoy it", "love the feeling").
