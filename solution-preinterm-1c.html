1C Listening: Problems, problems!

I can understand dialogues about personal problems.

Listening Scripts

Transcript 1.03 (Exercise 4)

Ben: Hi, Ellie. How are you? Ellie: Oh, I’m OK, thanks. Ben: You don’t sound very happy. Ellie: Well, to be honest, I’m a bit upset about the school show. It doesn’t look like I can be in it this year. Because a relative of mine is getting married – and the wedding is on the same night as the first performance. Ben: That’s bad luck! Ellie: I know.
Leo: Hi, Daisy. What are you up to? Daisy: I’m reading an email from my cousin in Canada. He’s planning to visit us next month! I can’t wait … Leo: That’s good news. But I didn’t think you got on with him that well. Daisy: I don’t. But he’s OK. Leo: I don’t understand. You seem really excited … Daisy: I know – because he’s bringing me a DVD of the new Twilight film! It isn’t in the shops here, only in America.
Molly: Hi, James. What’s wrong? James: Oh, nothing. I’m fine, really – just a bit worried. Molly: Why’s that? James: I’ve got a piano exam this afternoon and I don’t think I’m ready for it. I need to practise more.

Transcript 1.04 (Exercises 5 & 6)

Dialogue A: Sophia: Hi, Charlie. You don't look very happy. Is there a problem? Charlie: Well, yes. I just don’t know what to do. Sophia: Tell me about it. Charlie: ... I feel bad about leaving the team in the middle of the year. Sophia: Why don't you talk to your teammates about it? Charlie: I talked to them yesterday. They were really upset! They say they need me in the team. Sophia: Oh dear – I see your problem. What can I do? I don't know. Sorry!
Dialogue B: Maisie: Hi, Daniel. Daniel: Oh, hello Maisie. How are you? You don’t sound very happy! Maisie: ... Do you think I should tell the truth? Daniel: Hmm. No, I don't. Just say you went to the concert and enjoyed it. I mean, you don't want your aunt to be upset, do you? Maisie: No. I guess you’re right, Daniel.
Dialogue C: Emma: Hello, Luke. Can I ask you something? It's about my science project. Luke: Talk to Mr Woods, then. Ask for more time. Emma: ... So I don't want to ask for more time. Luke: No, I don’t think you should do that. Just talk to him, that’s my advice. Emma: I can't. You don't understand! I'm looking for science projects on the internet.
Dialogue D: George: I’ve got this problem with my Facebook page. Some people made comments. Alice: No, I don’t think you should do that. But you should delete the comments. George: OK. I can do that now. Thanks for the advice. But I still feel bad.
